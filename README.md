AI Personalized Finance & Health Optimization Platform

This project is an AI-driven platform designed to improve financial awareness and overall well-being of individuals. Many people struggle to manage their income, control expenses, and identify suitable government schemes. This platform solves that problem by providing intelligent, data-driven insights.

The system tracks user income and expenses, analyzes spending patterns, and provides personalized budgeting and savings recommendations. It also suggests suitable investment options based on user profiles and financial goals.

In addition, the platform recommends relevant government schemes based on eligibility such as income level, occupation, and demographics. A unique feature of this system is health optimization, where users receive personalized diet and lifestyle suggestions.

The goal is to create a single smart assistant that helps users make better financial decisions while also improving their health.
